1. Product Overview
מקלטים is a dark-humor arcade endless runner survival game where the player must reach a bomb shelter before the countdown ends.
The game reflects everyday Israeli life during sirens, turning stressful moments into absurd gameplay situations involving neighbors, building obstacles, and social awkwardness.
Players navigate through chaotic apartment buildings while avoiding delays caused by neighbors, pets, and everyday building problems.
The core experience focuses on fast, funny, replayable gameplay loops lasting 30–90 seconds.

2. Product Vision
Create a highly shareable, culturally authentic arcade game that captures the absurd humor of everyday life in Israel.
The game should feel:
Fast
Funny
Chaotic

Replayable
Target outcome: a viral indie browser game with strong cultural identity.

Primary Users

4. Problem Statement
There are very few games that represent authentic Israeli daily life and humor.
Existing casual games rarely include:
Local cultural references
Familiar social situations
Absurd neighborhood interactions
This game fills that gap by transforming shared cultural experiences into comedic gameplay.

5. Core Gameplay Loop
1️⃣ Siren starts
2️⃣ Countdown timer begins
3️⃣ Player runs toward the shelter
4️⃣ Player navigates obstacles and neighbors
5️⃣ Player either:
reaches the shelter (success)
fails to arrive in time (failure)
6️⃣ Player earns survival score
7️⃣ Player unlocks upgrades or retries
Game loop duration: 30–90 seconds
